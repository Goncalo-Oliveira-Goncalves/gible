§ V · The Oracle

FAQ

Common questions, answered.

What software do you use? ▾
I use DaVinci Resolve for video editing and Krita for graphic design and thumbnail creation. Both are industry-capable tools that let me produce high-quality work efficiently.
How long does delivery take? ▾
Turnaround depends on the scope of the project. Thumbnails are typically done within 24–48 hours. Video edits vary — I'll give you a clear timeline upfront so there are no surprises.
Do you offer revisions? ▾
Yes! I want you to be fully happy with the result. I include revisions in my process — just communicate clearly what you'd like changed and I'll get it sorted.
How do I pay? ▾
Payment details are discussed after we agree on the project scope. I work with common platforms — we'll figure out what works best for both of us.
Can I see samples before commissioning? ▾
Absolutely — the portfolio above shows real work. You can also reach out directly and I can share more specific examples relevant to your project type.
What kind of projects do you take on? ▾
Video edits (YouTube, Shorts, Reels), thumbnail design, UGC promo content, graphic design posters and more. If you're unsure, just ask — I'm flexible.
